MEMORY Map

Aleida Assman, one of the world’s most famous researchers of memory culture says:

“The future cannot simply be separated from the past.[…] We come to the future only through the past. Especially when that past is still felt in the present. Something needs to be done with it because the path to the future will remain simply blocked. After all, the goal is the future, not the past. […] It's transforming energy".

The purpose of the Šančiai memory map is to collect and to share images and narratives of our collective and individual history. It is based not only on the officially protected cultural heritage, but also on our memories, legends of the place, personal images, etc.

Here goes any text to explain what is memory map and how to fill the map.

- Select the point and place it in the map where is your location.

- If you make a mistake, you can drag it to another location on the map.

- Fill in the info of your memory and indicate the sources of information and the authors of the photos.

- After providing all the required information click the "submit" button.
