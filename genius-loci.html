Urbanization and civil society

This project is about the involvement of citizens in the planning of the territory of Šančiai district. It is funded by the EEA and Norwegian financial mechanisms and organised by Žemųjų Šančių community. Project time: 2020-2023.

Ed Carroll

Ed Carroll

Vision

An active civil society involved in decision-making.

Mision

Activate the participation of project target groups not involved in decision-making in civic activities and increase the number of users of digital tools that promote public participation in civic activities.

Goals and objetives

-To solve the problem of unsustainable urbanization in Kaunas Šančiai district that violates the interests of local residents.

-Create conditions for more active participation of citizens in democratic processes.

-Strengthen and enable active citizenship.
