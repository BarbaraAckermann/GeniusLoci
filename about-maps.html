Become a creator of Šančiai history and upload your information to the maps.

How to leave your note

1) CLICK THE BUTTON "CREATE THE MAP"

And select in the map the POINT that you want to mark a specific place (post office, yard, area, etc), an area (street, Nemunas coastline, etc.) o or a territory (Šančiai quarter or a set of quarters, a group of buildings, etc.).

2) MARK ON THE MAP

Mark in the map the POINT that you want to mark a specific place (post office, yard, area, etc), an area (street, Nemunas coastline, etc.) o or a territory (Šančiai quarter or a set of quarters, a group of buildings, etc.). If you made a mistake, drag the symbol to another location on the map.

3) FILL THE FORM

Provide information in the opened window. Enter all the details about that place that you want to share. You can write a description, define the type of place, add photos and links.

4) CONFIRMATION

Complete the form with some general info about yourself and Click the "Agree" button and confirm that you agree with data protection conditions. Personal data will only be used for urban research, statistical purposes and communication. The data is not made public.

5) SUBMIT

Click the "Submit" button to send your note to be reviewed.It might take some time to process the information, so please allow some time before it goes published. Thanks for collaborating into Memory Maps creation!

EXPLORE THE MAP

You can see all the marked places on the map. To see what is marked in them, click on the object you are interested in. We hope that this map will become not only a reference for creating a vision of the future of Šančiai, but also a means to get to know your area better.

The territory of Šančiai district and its community are an inseparable unique organism that builds its future based on the past and the present. The most important tool in creating the urban vision of Šančiai is the map. A map is a graphical model of a territory that summarizes complex and otherwise difficult-to-convey information. Without a map, no urban planning process is possible. The map can become not only a planning tool, but also a tool for understanding the territory and its history, a representative symbol, a tool for learning, a roadmap for the future, a repository of memory , etc. We are the creators of our own maps.

The map development process is enabled by you and KTU, a partner in the Genius Loci: Urbanization and Civil Society project.

The city is a developing system, for which the maintenance of its life is based on a balance between various interest groups such as community, business, politicians, administration, etc. An active participation of citizens in the planning of their environment is not only an expression of democracy and the right to live in a healthy and harmonious environment, but also a guarantee of the city's organic development. It helps both to preserve the neighbourhood’s character, to nourish diversity and to avoid unilateral decisions that satisfy only the interests of narrow groups.
